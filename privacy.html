Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Clonal Hematopoiesis Risk Score mobile application ("the App") handles information. The App is developed and maintained by Dae Hyun Lee, MD ("we," "us," or "our").

Information Collection and Use

No Personal Data Collection

The App is designed with privacy as a fundamental principle:

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information
  • No Patient Data: No patient health information is collected or stored
  • No Account Creation: The App does not require or support user accounts
  • No Cloud Storage: All calculations are performed locally on your device
  • No Analytics: We do not use any analytics or tracking services
  • No Advertising: The App contains no advertisements or marketing trackers

Local Storage

The App uses local device storage only for:

  • Saving your professional verification status
  • Storing your acceptance of terms and age verification
  • These preferences remain on your device and are never transmitted

Calculation Data

  • All medical calculations are performed entirely on your device
  • Input values for calculations are not saved after the calculation is complete
  • Results are displayed only during your current session
  • No calculation history is maintained

Data Security

Since we do not collect any data, there is no risk of your personal or patient information being compromised through our servers. All app functionality operates locally on your device.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or platforms that could collect user data.

Children's Privacy

The App is rated 17+ and is intended only for use by healthcare professionals. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 17 years of age.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Compliance

Medical Disclaimer

This App is a clinical decision support tool designed for healthcare professionals only. It does not provide medical advice and should not replace professional clinical judgment. All results must be interpreted by qualified healthcare professionals in the context of complete clinical assessment.
