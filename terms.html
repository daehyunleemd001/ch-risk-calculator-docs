3. Description of Service

The App provides:

  • Implementation of the Clonal Hematopoiesis Risk Score (CHRS) based on Weeks et al. (NEJM Evidence, 2023)
  • Risk calculations for therapy-related myeloid neoplasms in patients with clonal hematopoiesis
